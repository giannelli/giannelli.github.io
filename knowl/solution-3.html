Proposition5.2.2

\(A'\) is a subspace if and only if \(\vec{b} \in A\).

Proof

If \(A \subset \mathbb{R}3\) be a subspace of rank 2 then it is a plane through the origin. If \(\vec{x} \in A\) then \begin{equation*}\vec{v} = t_1 \vec{v_1} + t_2 \vec{v_2}\end{equation*} for direction vectors \(\vec{v_1},\vec{v_2}\).
